What a pin means

Different kinds of connection.

Project hub

A site coordinating study activity, sampling, laboratory work, analysis or training.

Study setting

A location represented in a dataset or field programme; not necessarily a permanent institutional partnership.

Research collaborator

A longer-term scientific relationship spanning projects, papers, methods or supervision.

Training and sequencing link

A site contributing specialist infrastructure, workshops, mentoring or analytical support.

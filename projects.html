Project architecture

Each programme links five layers.

  1. A biological or public-health question
  2. A sampling frame that represents the relevant population
  3. Laboratory methods that are portable and comparable
  4. Analysis that handles population structure and uncertainty
  5. Outputs that partners can reuse for surveillance, training or intervention
